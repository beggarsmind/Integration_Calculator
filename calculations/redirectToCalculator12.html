About Hyperbolic Functions:

Hyperbolic functions are related to the exponential function:

  • cosh(x) = (ex + e-x)/2
  • sinh(x) = (ex - e-x)/2

Interesting fact: cosh(x) ≥ 1 for all real x, and its graph forms a catenary curve (the shape of a hanging chain).
