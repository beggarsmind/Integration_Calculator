About Inverse Tangent Function:

The inverse tangent function (also called arctan or tan-1) has the following properties:

  • Domain: All real numbers
  • Range: (-π/2, π/2) or (-90°, 90°)
  • If tan(θ) = x, then tan-1(x) = θ

This integral appears in many applications including signal processing and statistics.
